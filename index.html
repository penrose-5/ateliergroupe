OPENING

Given our contemporary condition of overwhelming information, ATELIERGROUPE aims to define precise operational strategies to achieve a voluntary, deliberate, and non-random transformation of our environment. In order to organize the project, ATELIERGROUPE is taking notes from architecture *, using it as a tool able to define the context and formalize the given. Eventually, ATELIERGROUPE’s projects are intentionally cultural, as mise-en-scène in relation to the territory it engages, defined by both its ambitious character and the obstacles it has to tackle.
