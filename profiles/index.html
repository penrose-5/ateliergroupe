The room is rectangular; so is the project: a wooden mask. It outlines an entrance, erases another one, yet nothing has changed. It defines a room ready to pretend it is its double: Archizoom’s exhibition room. Negotiation takes place. The partition wall ensures the permanence of an existing qualitative flexible space by responding to two specific problems: poor acoustic and a lack of means of organization. Freeing itself from its primary function, it becomes the generator of an architecture that divides and organizes by regulating the intensity of the coexistence of different programs. Project Room is about architecture.
